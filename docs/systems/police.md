# Police & Heat System

## Heat Levels
0 – Free roam
1 – Patrols
2 – Active pursuit
3 – Roadblocks
4 – Heavy units
5 – All-out hunt

## Heat Increase
- Illegal races
- Collisions
- Speeding near police

## Heat Decrease
- Escape pursuit
- Time hidden
- Safehouse (future)

## Police States
- Patrol
- Chase
- Search
- Return
